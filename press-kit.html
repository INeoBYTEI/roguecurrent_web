Project description

Project Overdrive is a high-speed sci-fi action game where players build momentum through fluid movement and high-octane combat while facing colossal creatures. Speed is both a weapon and a resource, pushing players to stay in motion, tear through hostile swarms, and strike at enemies far larger than themselves.

Studio boilerplate

Rogue Current is an independent game studio formed by a team of developers and artists with a shared love of ambitious games, expressive worlds, and mechanics that feel impossible to put down. The studio is currently developing its first title, Project Overdrive, a high-speed sci-fi action game built around momentum, movement, and battles against colossal creatures.
