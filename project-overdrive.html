Overview

Project Overdrive is a high-speed sci-fi action game built around momentum. Tear through hostile swarms, shift into higher gears, and transform movement into power while battling the colossal Spineback.

Every kill feeds your speed. Every gear makes you deadlier. Chain together parkour, attacks, and evasive movement to reach Overdrive and launch devastating assaults against enemies many times your size.

Project Overdrive is the first title from Rogue Current and is currently in active development.
